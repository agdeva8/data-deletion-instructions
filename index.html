Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request the deletion of your personal data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at privacy@yourdomain.com.
  2. Include your full name, email address, and any other identifying information associated with your account.
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

For any questions, feel free to contact us at privacy@yourdomain.com.
